Dig This Volleyball is a community committed to developing and promoting the sport of volleyball from beginners to advanced player. Our goal is to provide an environment where we can support each other in the development of skills and sportsmanship, while providing a fun and nurturing place for players to reach their maximum playing potential.

Open Play - Indoor

September 2016 - June 2017

Open Play for beginners, intermediate and advanced Players.

IMPORTANT - Skill Level Required for our open play. Know how to rotate on the court, serve, pass, set, spike and block. Know how to hold your position on the court during defense and offense. This is meant for players that have experience playing. Rules will be enforced to protect all players from safety and fun. Recreational players and beginners can practice on the side or come watch for FREE. We also have individual coaching, please ask!
